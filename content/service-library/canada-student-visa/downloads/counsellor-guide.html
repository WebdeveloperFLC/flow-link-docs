Counsellor Guide — Session Companion

One-page companion to run a Canada study-permit counselling session end to end.

Before the session

  • Confirm enquiry source and student profile (age, academics, budget, goals)
  • Pull the live processing time for the client's country
  • Have the official links ready (study permit, proof of funds, PAL/TAL)

During the session — cover in order

  • Fit & goals (why Canada, why this course, career plan)
  • Eligibility snapshot: LOA/DLI, PAL/TAL or exemption, funds, language
  • Money talk: proof of funds CAD $22,895 (confirm) + tuition + travel; govt fees CAD $235
  • Honest expectation-setting: we prepare; IRCC decides — no guarantees
  • Work rights: student 24 hrs/week in term; spouse SOWP only for grad/professional
  • Timeline & cap: apply early, PAL allocations can fill
  • Next steps & document checklist hand-off

Red lines to flag immediately

  • Funds not liquid / arranged late
  • Undisclosed prior refusal
  • Program/level mismatch with past study
  • Final-term student promising spouse work rights

Close

  • Agree next action + date
  • Issue personalised document checklist
  • Log counselling note
