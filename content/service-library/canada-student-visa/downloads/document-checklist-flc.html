Document Checklist (FLC)

Suggested documents by category — tailor to the client on enrolment. Not all are compulsory.

Suggested document sets by category — NOT compulsory. This is the starting binder used to build a complete visa file; the list is tailored to each client on enrolment. Acceptable proof is defined by IRCC — confirm on the official document pages.

Identity Core

  • Passport (all used pages + bio page)
  • National ID
  • Passport-size photographs (IRCC spec)
  • Birth certificate

Academic Core

  • 10th grade certificate & marksheet
  • 12th grade certificate & marksheet
  • Graduation/diploma degree
  • Consolidated transcripts / marksheets
  • Backlog / migration / provisional certificate (if applicable)
  • English test result (IELTS/PTE/TOEFL)

Admission Core

  • Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from a DLI
  • PAL/TAL (or exemption proof)
  • Tuition fee payment receipt
  • Quebec CAQ (if studying in Quebec)

Financial / Proof of funds Core

  • Bank statements (last 4–6 months)
  • GIC certificate (if SDS)
  • Education loan sanction letter
  • Scholarship letter (if any)
  • Sponsor affidavit + relationship proof (if sponsored)
  • Income tax returns / Form 16
  • Proof of liquid assets / FDs

Employment proof (if working) If applicable

  • Employment / appointment letter
  • Recent salary slips (3–6 months)
  • Experience / relieving letters
  • Employer ID / business card

Business proof (if self-employed) If applicable

  • Business registration / license
  • GST / tax registration
  • Business bank statements
  • Office address proof
  • Company profile / brochure
  • Audited financials / ITR

Marriage / relationship proof (if spouse involved) If applicable

  • Marriage certificate
  • Wedding photographs
  • Communication / chat history
  • Joint address / utility proof
  • Joint bank account (if any)
  • Invitation cards

Study plan / intent Core

  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Gap explanation letter (if applicable)
  • Career progression / future plan note

Supporting (conditional) If applicable

  • Medical exam confirmation (if required)
  • Biometrics receipt
  • Prior visa refusal disclosure (if any)
  • Custodianship declaration (minors)
Acceptable proof is defined by IRCC: IRCC: Get the right documents · IRCC: Guide 5269 — study permit (outside Canada) · IRCC: Proof of financial support
