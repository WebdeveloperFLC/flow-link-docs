# CRM Overview

This CRM is used by Future Link Consultants (study-abroad & immigration agency). Main areas accessible from the left sidebar:

- **Dashboard** (`/`) — landing page with key stats.
- **Leads** (`/leads`) — warm leads being worked by counselors.
- **Cold Pool** (`/leads/cold`) — unassigned/cold leads waiting for outreach.
- **+ New Lead** (`/leads/new`) — create a lead manually.
- **Clients** (`/clients`) — converted leads / active clients.
- **+ New Client** (`/clients/new`) — register a new client.
- **Messages** (`/messages`) — unified inbox for client and internal chats.
- **Telecaller** (`/telecaller`) — outbound calling queue, today's calls, lists.
- **Course Finder** (`/course-finder`) — search programs across institutions.
- **Workflows** (`/templates`) — workflow templates.
- **Forms Library** (`/forms-library`) — questionnaires & dynamic forms (admin).
- **Letter Templates** (`/letter-templates`) — letter/email templates (admin).
- **Activity** (`/activity`) — system-wide audit log.
- **Team Access** (`/team-access`) — who has access to which client.
- **Team & Roles** (`/users`) — manage users and permissions (admin).
- **Offers & Discounts** (`/offers-admin`) — promotional offers (admin).
- **Settle Abroad** (`/assessment-admin`) — assessment & eligibility tool.
- **Masters** (`/masters`) — branches, departments, services catalogue.
- **Settings** (`/settings`) — workspace settings.
- **Institutions** (`/institutions`) — university/institution database.
- **Commissions** (`/commissions`) — commission tracking.
- **Digital Success Hub** (`/digital-success`) — marketing content library.
- **Accounting** (`/accounting/*`) — full accounting module (journals, AP, AR, reports, tax, petty cash, etc.).

Roles: `admin`, `counselor`, `telecaller`, `documentation`, `viewer`. Admins see everything; other roles see modules per their permissions (managed in Team & Roles).
