# CRM Overview

This CRM is used by Future Link Consultants (study-abroad & immigration agency). Main areas in the left sidebar:

- **Dashboard** (`/`), **Leads** (`/leads`), **Cold Pool** (`/leads/cold`), **+ New Lead** (`/leads/new`).
- **Clients** (`/clients`). New clients are registered from **+ New Lead** via **Register as Client**.
- **Messages** (`/messages`), **Telecaller** (`/telecaller`), **Course Finder** (`/course-finder`).
- **Workflows** (`/templates`), **Forms Library** (`/forms-library`), **Letter Templates** (`/letter-templates`).
- **Activity** (`/activity`), **Team Access** (`/team-access`), **Team & Roles** (`/users`).
- **Offers & Discounts** (`/offers-admin`), **Settle Abroad** (`/assessment-admin`), **Masters** (`/masters`), **Settings** (`/settings`).
- **Institutions** (`/institutions`), **Commissions** (`/commissions`), **Digital Success Hub** (`/digital-success`).
- **Accounting** (`/accounting/*`).

Roles: `admin`, `counselor`, `telecaller`, `documentation`, `viewer`. Admins see everything; others see modules per their permissions (managed in Team & Roles).
