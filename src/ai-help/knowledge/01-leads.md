# Leads (Warm & Cold)

**Warm Leads** (`/leads`): leads assigned to a counselor and being actively worked. Click a row to open `/leads/:id` for full detail, timeline, notes, calls, and conversion actions.

**Cold Pool** (`/leads/cold`): unassigned leads. Telecallers/admins pick or are bulk-assigned leads. Use **Import Cold Leads** to bulk-upload from CSV (admin only).

**Create a lead**: sidebar → **+ New Lead** → fill name, phone (with country code), email, interested countries/services, source.

**Register as client**: on the lead form click **Register as Client** (or from lead detail → **Register as Client**) — additional sections unlock for academics, family, and invoice. Saves to `/clients/:id` and marks the lead converted.

**Service tabs**: leads filterable by service (Study, Immigration, Visit visa, etc.) via the top tabs on the leads list.

**Bulk distribute (telecaller)**: telecaller page → Lists tab → select a list → Bulk Distribute to assign chunks to multiple telecallers.

Common questions:
- "Why can't I see a lead?" — check if it's assigned to you or in a list you have access to (ask admin to share via Team Access).
- "How do I import cold leads?" — Cold Pool → Import button (admin only).
