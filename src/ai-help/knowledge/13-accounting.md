# Accounting

Full accounting module at `/accounting/*`. Requires accounting access (`/accounting/access`).

Sections: Overview, Journal entries, Chart of accounts, Bank accounts, Petty cash, Inter-company, Reimbursements, Statement reconciliation, AP — Bills, AR — Invoices, Vendors, Clients, Documents & OCR, Approvals, Reports (P&L, BS, Cash Flow, Trial Balance, GL, Consolidated), Tax & compliance, Fraud & audit, AI assistant, Settings (Entities, Users, Access management).

Multi-entity: switch entity via the dropdown in the AI composer or top bar of accounting pages.
