# Digital Success Hub

`/digital-success` — central library of marketing content for branches to share with prospects.

Tabs: **Common**, **Country**, **Institution**, **Service Category**, **Front Desk**, **Google Reviews**.

Each item is a link (Drive/OneDrive) or uploaded file, tagged by type (promo_video, reel, poster, document). Branches can be notified when new content is added (Notify Branches).

**Google Reviews**: track Google reviews each counselor brings in. When adding a review item, pick credited team member, review date, branch, and client. Panel shows monthly tracker with By-Counselor and By-Branch tables.

**Copy link** copies shareable URL to clipboard.
