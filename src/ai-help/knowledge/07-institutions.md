# Institutions

`/institutions` — partner universities/colleges. Requires "institutions" module permission.

- **List** — search and filter.
- **Detail** (`/institutions/:id`) — overview, programs, agreements, commissions, promotions, claims, campaigns, AI suggestions.
- **Course Review** (`/institutions/review`) — review newly imported/staged courses before publishing.
- **AI Suggestions** (`/institutions/suggestions`) — AI-generated suggestions (missing fields, expiring agreements, etc.).

**Agreements**: contract validity per institution. **Commissions**: commission models that feed the Commissions module.
