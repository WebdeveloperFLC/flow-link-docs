# Offers & Discounts

`/offers-admin` (admin) — define promotional offers (e.g. "10% off application fee", "Free IELTS coaching for August intake").

Each offer: title, description, eligibility (country/service/institution), discount amount/%, validity dates, status.

**Apply to client**: client detail → Offers tab → select applicable offer → appears on client portal (`/portal/offers`) too.
