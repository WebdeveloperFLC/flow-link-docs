# Telecaller

`/telecaller` tabs:
- **My Queue** — leads assigned to you to call today.
- **Today's Calls** — calls scheduled or completed today.
- **Inbox** — incoming/missed calls.
- **Lists** — campaign lists with bulk-distribute, import.

**Make a call**: phone icon next to a lead → Call Drawer with browser-phone (if telephony connected at `/settings/telephony`) or shows number to dial manually.

**Post-call notes**: after a call ends, log outcome (interested, not interested, callback, etc.) and notes. Outcomes update lead status automatically.

**Telephony setup**: admin → `/settings/telephony-integration` to connect provider credentials.
