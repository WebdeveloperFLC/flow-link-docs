# Templates & Letter Templates

- **Workflows** (`/templates`) — reusable workflow templates (sequences of steps, tasks).
- **Letter Templates** (`/letter-templates`, admin) — letter/email templates with merge fields (`{{client.name}}`, `{{program.title}}`).
- **Questionnaire emails** (`/settings/questionnaire-emails`) — emails sent when inviting clients to fill questionnaires.
- **Forms Library** (`/forms-library`) — build dynamic forms/questionnaires (admin).

From a client's Letters tab: pick template → preview → generate a PDF letter saved to the client's binder.
