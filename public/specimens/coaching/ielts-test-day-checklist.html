IELTS Test Day Checklist

Future Link Consultants · Give to every student before exam day

Confirm module (Academic / General Training / UKVI) matches visa or university requirement. Bring the same valid passport used at registration. Exam fee was paid at booking — not to FLC on test day.

Night before / morning

Passport valid and matches registration name exactly
Test confirmation email / receipt saved (digital or print)
Correct test center address and reporting time noted
Module confirmed: Academic ☐   GT ☐   UKVI ☐
Good sleep — avoid heavy last-minute cramming

Do's on test day

Arrive 30–45 minutes early
Bring passport original only
Check headphones in Listening
Manage Writing time (Task 2 > Task 1)
Report tech problems immediately

Don'ts on test day

No phone or smartwatch in room
No talking during the test
No memorized template essays
Don't argue about score with examiner
Don't leave seat without permission

After the test

Note expected result date (CD-IELTS ~3–5 days; paper ~13 days)
Send TRF screenshot to FLC counselor when available
Update admissions / visa team with L/R/W/S breakdown
