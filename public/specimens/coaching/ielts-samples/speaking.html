Speaking · Specimen

Part 2 — Cue card practice

FLC training specimen. 1 minute prep · 2 minutes speaking.

Part 1 warm-up (examples)

  • Do you study or work?
  • What do you like about your hometown?
  • How often do you use English outside class?

Part 2 — Cue card

Describe a skill you would like to learn in the future.

You should say:

  • what the skill is
  • why you want to learn it
  • how you would learn it
  • and explain how this skill might help you in the future
Student has 1 minute to prepare notes, then speaks for up to 2 minutes without interruption.

Sample 2-minute structure

  1. Opening: Name the skill (e.g. public speaking / data analysis)
  2. Why: Career or study goal linked to abroad plans
  3. How: Course, mentor, daily practice
  4. Future benefit: University presentations, job interviews
  5. Close: One sentence summary

Part 3 follow-up (examples)

  • What skills are most valued in your country today?
  • Should schools teach more life skills?
  • Is it easier for young people to learn new skills now than in the past?
