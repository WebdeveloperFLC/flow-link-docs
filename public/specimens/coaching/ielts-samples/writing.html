Writing Task 1 (20 minutes)

The bar chart below shows the number of international students enrolled at four universities in 2020 and 2023.

University20202023
Northbridge1,2001,650
Westfield9801,100
Riverdale1,4501,420
Summit7601,050

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant. Write at least 150 words.

Band 6 outline: Overview + one paragraph per time period, some figures, limited comparison.
Band 7 outline: Clear overview (overall increase except Riverdale) · grouped comparisons · accurate data · less listing.

Writing Task 2 (40 minutes)

Some people believe that universities should focus only on academic subjects, while others think they should also teach practical skills such as communication and teamwork.

Discuss both views and give your own opinion. Write at least 250 words.

Suggested plan:

  • Intro: paraphrase + thesis (balance both)
  • Body 1: academic focus — research, depth, credentials
  • Body 2: practical skills — employability, collaboration
  • Conclusion: integrated curriculum best for most students
