Arrival Checklist

Port-of-entry readiness — what the student carries and does on arrival.

Carry in hand luggage (originals)

  • Passport with valid visa/eTA
  • Port-of-entry (POE) letter of introduction
  • Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from the DLI
  • Proof of funds documents
  • PAL/TAL (or exemption proof)
  • Tuition payment receipt
  • Medical exam confirmation (if applicable)
  • Accommodation details

At the port of entry

  • Present documents to the border officer
  • Answer questions about your study plan honestly
  • Receive your printed study permit — check name, DLI, conditions, expiry
  • Confirm work conditions are printed if you intend to work

First 48 hours

  • Activate SIM / connectivity
  • Confirm accommodation
  • Note campus international-student office hours
Sources: IRCC: Prepare for arrival · IRCC: Get the right documents
