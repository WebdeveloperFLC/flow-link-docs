✅Who can apply — quick eligibility

  • A Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI) in Canada.
  • Proof of funds — enough to cover living costs (CAD $22,895), first-year tuition, and travel.
  • A PAL/TAL (Provincial/Territorial Attestation Letter) if required — though Master's & PhD students at public DLIs are now exempt.
  • Genuine student intent — a clear study plan and ties to your home country.
  • Clean admissibility — medical exam and police certificate where required.

🛫The journey — step by step

  1. Counselling & course selection — pick the right DLI and program for your goals and budget.
  2. Admission — secure your Letter of Acceptance and PAL/TAL.
  3. Funds & documents — arrange proof of funds and prepare your file.
  4. Apply to IRCC — submit online, pay fees, give biometrics, complete medical if required.
  5. Decision & travel — receive your approval, then fly to Canada and collect your study permit at the port of entry.
